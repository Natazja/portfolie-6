About Atelier September

Atelier September is founded by chef Frederik Bille Brahe and located in the centre of Copenhagen and a little north; in Hellerup.

Breakfast, lunch and cakes as well as warm and cold drinks are served throughout the day and the ambience is warm, vibrant and welcoming.
